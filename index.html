About LithionX

India’s EV and solar battery demand is growing rapidly. 80% of lithium-ion battery packs are imported from China, which are costly, unsafe, and difficult to repair. There's a lack of local assembling units and trained technicians.

LithionX establishes local lithium-ion battery assembling units, providing custom packs for EVs, solar energy, drones, and e-bikes. With advanced BMS, our packs are safe, high-performing, affordable, and repairable.

Business Model: B2B supply, B2C sales, services, and training. Market: ₹60,000+ crore in 2025, reaching ₹1.5 lakh crore by 2030.

Value: High performance, 30% cheaper, safe, customizable, sustainable. USP: Local manufacturing with global standards.

EV Market Trends in India (2025)

  • In FY 2024-25, over 2 million EVs were sold in India, marking a 15.68% year-over-year growth, with e-two-wheelers dominating at 59.4% of sales.
  • The Indian EV market is projected to reach $54.41 billion in 2025, growing from 5% of the auto sector in 2024 to 30% by 2030.
  • By 2025, EV sales could surpass 700,000 units annually, driven by government incentives, local manufacturing, and infrastructure development.
  • Production of battery-powered passenger vehicles expected to soar by 140% to approximately 301,400 units in 2025.
  • Key focus on two-wheelers and three-wheelers, with top states like Uttar Pradesh, Maharashtra, and Karnataka leading adoption.
  • Government's EV policy emphasizes incentives, charging networks, and localized production to accelerate the EV revolution.
